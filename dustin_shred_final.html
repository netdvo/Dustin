Meal prep
1. Chicken rice bowls
5 portions · backbone of the plan
38g protein45g carbs390 kcal
▾
Ingredients
2 lbs chicken breast · 2.5 cups white rice (dry) · broccoli · garlic powder · paprika · salt · olive oil spray
Method
Season chicken with garlic + paprika. Bake 400°F for 22 min. Cook rice in bulk. Steam broccoli. Slice and divide into 5 containers. Refrigerates 5 days.
2. Tuna + egg white muffins
Makes 12 · zero-carb snack
28g protein2g carbs155 kcal
▾
Ingredients
2 cans tuna (drained) · 8 egg whites · 2 whole eggs · diced bell pepper · spinach · salt · pepper · hot sauce
Method
Mix all ingredients. Pour into greased muffin tin. Bake 375°F for 18–20 min. Serve 2–3 per meal. Stores 5 days.
3. Salmon + quinoa + asparagus
Serves 4 · omega-3 dinner
38g protein38g carbs430 kcal
▾
Ingredients
4×5oz salmon fillets · 1.5 cups quinoa (dry) · 1 bunch asparagus · lemon · garlic · olive oil · dill
Method
Season salmon with lemon + garlic. Bake 400°F for 15 min. Cook quinoa with broth. Roast asparagus 12 min. Divide into 4 containers. Stores 4 days.
4. Turkey + sweet potato hash
Serves 4 · pre-workout power
34g protein40g carbs360 kcal
▾
Ingredients
1.5 lbs lean ground turkey · 3 sweet potatoes (cubed) · 1 onion · bell pepper · spinach · cumin · chili powder · garlic
Method
Brown turkey in pan. Sauté sweet potato + veggies 10 min. Combine, add spinach, cook 3 min. Divide into 4 containers.
5. Greek yogurt overnight oats
Make 5 jars every Sunday
30g protein52g carbs375 kcal
▾
Ingredients
½ cup oats · ¾ cup non-fat Greek yogurt · ½ cup almond milk · 1 scoop protein powder · 1 tbsp chia seeds · berries
Method
Mix all in a jar. Refrigerate overnight. Top with berries in the morning. Make 5 jars every Sunday.
